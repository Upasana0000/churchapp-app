Who We Are

Anglicare NT is a faith-informed agency, proud of its traditions as an NGO (non-government organisation) and a long-term territory based community service organisation that continues to adapt and respond to the needs of our communities.

Anglicare NT was formed by the Anglican Church in the Northern Territory in response to identified community social needs. From the beginning, services have embraced all Territorians; no one is denied the respect, justice, and community of our work. Similarly, our staff reflect the full diversity of our community.

Our Values

Our values of respect, kindness, hope, fairness, and integrity, as well as many other life-affirming values are expressed in many religious traditions and philosophies. In seeking to live and strengthen our values at Anglicare NT we will reference Christian thought and draw on the resources of the Anglican Church. All staff who agree to work at Anglicare NT are asked to respect the Christian foundation and values of Anglicare NT.

Our Foundations

Anglicare NT is a local organisation providing responsive services and targeted advocacy for the people of the Northern Territory and their diverse communities. We are a separately incorporated agency of the Anglican Diocese of the NT.
